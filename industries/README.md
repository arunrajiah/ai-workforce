# 🏭 Industry Cross-Index

The [departments](../departments) are the primary map. This index re-slices the
same open-source apps by **industry**, so you can find what's most relevant to
your vertical fast. Each entry links back to its full card in the department it
lives in.

This index lists **AI-native** tools only (LLM / AI-agent at their core), plus
[Veska](../apps/veska/) — an AI-native back office for SMBs — as the back-office
layer across every vertical.

---

## Healthcare

| Project | What it does | Full card |
|---|---|---|
| [Onyx](../departments/support/README.md) | Private RAG/AI Q&A over clinical protocols, policies & guidelines | [Support](../departments/support/README.md) |
| [AnythingLLM](../departments/legal/README.md) | Local AI chat/RAG over patient docs & internal knowledge (keep PHI on-prem) | [Legal](../departments/legal/README.md) |
| [Veska](../apps/veska/) | AI-native back office (scheduling, billing ops, records admin) for clinics | [apps/veska](../apps/veska/) |

> ⚕️ Healthcare data is regulated. Self-host, keep PHI on your own infra, and get compliance sign-off.
