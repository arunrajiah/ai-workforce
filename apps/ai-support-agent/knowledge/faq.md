# Sample Knowledge Base

Replace the files in this `knowledge/` folder with your own docs (Markdown or
`.txt`). The agent re-indexes them on startup.

## Resetting your password

To reset your password, click "Forgot password?" on the login screen and enter
your account email. We'll send a reset link that stays valid for 60 minutes. If
it doesn't arrive, check your spam folder or contact support to verify your email
is correct.

## Refund policy

We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked. Refunds
are issued to the original payment method and take 5–10 business days to appear.
Annual plans cancelled after 30 days are prorated for the unused months.

## Supported plans and billing

We offer Free, Pro ($29/mo), and Team ($99/mo) plans. Billing is monthly or
annual (annual saves 20%). You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from
Settings → Billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current cycle.

## Data export and deletion

You can export all your data as JSON or CSV from Settings → Data. To permanently
delete your account and all associated data, use Settings → Danger Zone. Deletion
is irreversible and completes within 24 hours.

## Service status and uptime

Our target uptime is 99.9%. Live status and incident history are published on our
status page. Team-plan customers can subscribe to SMS incident alerts.
